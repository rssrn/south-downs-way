## Brighton Options (Thu 30 Apr 2026)

### Boutique Budget / Character B&Bs

#### Sea Spray Brighton ⭐
- **Type:** Boutique B&B in Grade II Regency townhouse, themed rooms
- **Price:** From ~£72/night
- **Breakfast:** Included (varied menu inc. vegan)
- **Address:** New Steine, Brighton BN1 1PR (seafront, near Palace Pier)
- **Website:** seaspraybrighton.co.uk
- **Notes:** Themed rooms (Elvis, Arabian Nights, etc.), lounge bar. Rated 8.7 Booking.com, couples rate location 9.5/10. 15 min walk from station.

#### Strawberry Fields
- **Type:** B&B in two Victorian townhouses
- **Price:** En-suite double from ~£55; shared bathroom from ~£45
- **Breakfast:** Not included (hot baps delivery £5pp)
- **Address:** 6–7 New Steine, Brighton BN2 1PB (near seafront)
- **Website:** strawberryfieldshotel.com
- **Notes:** Stylish, great value, very central. 10% off midweek + 10% direct booking (may stack). Sea view rooms available. 15 min from station.

#### Paskins Town House
- **Type:** Art deco B&B, 17 rooms, eco-friendly pioneer
- **Price:** From ~£65/night
- **Breakfast:** Included — outstanding organic/local menu, 13 cooked options inc. vegan/veggie
- **Address:** 18–19 Charlotte Street, Brighton BN2 1AG (Kemptown)
- **Website:** paskins.co.uk
- **Notes:** Award-winning green credentials. That breakfast is a real selling point after a day's walking. Some rooms small; occasional thin wall complaints. Bags of character. 15 min from station.

#### Amsterdam Hotel Brighton
- **Type:** Hotel, 24 rooms, seafront with bar/cabaret
- **Price:** From ~£65–85/night
- **Address:** 11–12 Marine Parade, Brighton BN2 1TL (opposite Palace Pier)
- **Website:** amsterdamhotelbrighton.com
- **Notes:** Quirky, fun, very Brighton. Refurbished bar. Worth phoning for Thu 30 Apr price.

#### Motel Schmotel
- **Type:** Budget B&B
- **Price:** From ~£46/night
- **Address:** 37 Russell Square, Brighton (central, quiet square)
- **Notes:** Bright white rooms, good breakfasts (vegan/GF options), complimentary snacks. £100 damage deposit required on arrival (refunded at checkout). Mixed recent reviews — verify before booking. 10 min from station.

### Recommendation

**Paskins Town House (~£65 inc. breakfast)** or **Sea Spray (~£72 inc. breakfast)** are the sweet spot — real character, great breakfast included, seafront area, under £75. Strawberry Fields is cheapest if you don't mind no breakfast.
