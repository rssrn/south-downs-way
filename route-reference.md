## River Crossings
- **River Meon** ~mile 12.5 (Exton)
- **River Arun** ~mile 48.5 (Amberley)
- **River Adur** ~mile 62 (Upper Beeding)
- **River Ouse** ~mile 83 (Southease)
- **River Cuckmere** ~mile 90 (Alfriston)

## Rail Bail-out Points
Stations on or near the trail (useful for emergencies or shortening):
- Petersfield (mile ~25.5, 2.5mi off trail)
- Amberley (mile 48.5, ON trail)
- Hassocks (mile ~68.5, 1.5mi off trail)
- Lewes (mile ~72.5 via Blackcap or ~79.5 via Kingston, 3.5/2.5mi off)
- Southease (mile 83, ON trail)

## Key A-road Crossings (also bail-out / taxi points)
- A286 at Cocking Hill (mile 36.5)
- A285 near Bignor Hill (mile ~44)
- A24 at Washington (mile 55)
- A23 at Pyecombe (mile 68)
- A27 at Housedean Farm (mile 76)
